Analysts predict that the Eras Tour will likely exceed $1 billion in revenue by next March as Taylor Swift continues her international performances. If this projection is accurate, she will set a new record for the highest-grossing tour in music history, surpassing Elton John's farewell tour, which ended earlier this summer with a total of $939 million, according to TIME .

The tour began in Glendale, Arizona on March 17, 2023 and is scheduled to end in Vancouver, Canada on December 8, 2024, with a total of 152 shows across 54 cities.
